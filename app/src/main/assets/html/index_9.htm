THE HOLY PROPHET

God has sent many prophets for the guidance of mankind. They all taught us to be good and to do good. Our Holy Prophet Muhammad (Peace be upon him) was the last of the prophets. He was born in 571 A. D. in Mecca. The people of Mecca liked him. They highly admired his truthfulness, honesty and sense of duty. They gave him the title of "Al-Amin" which means "the trustworthy". He received God's message at the age of 40, and began to preach Islam. He told the people of Mecca not to worship idols but the One God who is the creator of the entire universe. The people of Mecca, who worshiped idols, turned against him. They became his enemies. They did not want to preach Islam. The Holy Prophet left Mecca with his followers and went to Medina. The people of Medina received him with open arms. They were very happy to see the Prophet of God.
